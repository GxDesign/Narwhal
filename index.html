Learn to CodeWhile You Sleep

Narwhal uses sound waves - specifically the mating calls of magical narwhals - to help you absorb complex programming concepts.

Dive In

Icebreaking Technology

Ever hit an iceberg while programming and decided to sleep on it, only to wake up groggy and nowhere closer to a solution? Well, we haven't, but we hear it sucks. Extensive research has shown that the mating calls of Narwhals, when played backwards, contain frequencies that can significantly boost memory, a truly unforgettable auditory experience. Thats why we came up with Narwhal!

Narwhal takes those frequencies and overlays audiobooks, available across all coding languages, onto them to help you retain information while you sleep.
